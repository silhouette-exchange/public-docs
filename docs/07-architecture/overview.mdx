# Architecture Overview

Silhouette is directly integrated with both HyperCore and the HyperEVM.

- The **Silhouette UI** enables actions directly on HyperCore, interfaces with Silhouette's smart contract & TEE, and manages encryption keys.<br />
- The **Silhouette smart contract**, deployed on the HyperEVM, is the primary guardian of the platform.<br />
- A **Trusted Execution Environment (TEE)** hosts the matching engine and linkages between HyperCore and HyperEVM.
