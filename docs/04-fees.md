# Fee Structure

:::info 
**Current Status: Zero Fees**

Silhouette is currently operating with zero trading fees during the initial launch phase. This applies to all trading activity on the platform.

When fees are introduced, you'll receive advance notice through our official channels.
:::

---

## Standard Fee Structure

When activated, Silhouette will operate on a volume-based tier system. Your tier is determined by your total trading volume over the trailing 30 days.

**Maker fees** are charged when you add liquidity to the order book by placing limit orders that don't immediately match.

**Taker fees** are charged when you remove liquidity from the order book by placing orders that match immediately.

At volume tiers above $500M, maker fees are eliminated entirely to incentivise deep liquidity provision.

## Referral Programme

The referral programme is currently inactive during the zero-fee launch phase.

When activated, the programme will offer:

**For Referrers**
- Earn rebates on trading fees generated by referred traders
- Requires minimum $10,000 in personal trading volume to qualify
- Each referred trader can contribute up to $1B in lifetime rebates

**For Referred Traders**
- Receive fee discounts on your first $25M in trading volume
- Discounts apply automatically when using a referral code

Specific rates and additional details will be announced when the programme launches.

---

## How Volume is Calculated

- Volume is measured in USD equivalent across all trading pairs
- Calculated on a rolling 30-day basis
- Your tier automatically adjusts as your volume changes
- Both shielded and unshielded trades count towards your volume

---

## Why Fees Matter

Fees fund the infrastructure that keeps Silhouette operational: trusted execution environments, ongoing security audits, and platform development.

Unlike centralised venues, our fee structure is transparent from day one. You decide what the market learns from your trades. We're clear about what executing that discretion costs.

For questions about fee structure or billing, reach out through our official support channels.
