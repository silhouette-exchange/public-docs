Naked Trading on Silhouette routes orders directly to Hyperliquid via [Builder Codes](https://hyperliquid.gitbook.io/hyperliquid-docs/trading/builder-codes). Trades are not private when using Naked Trading. Perpetuals and spot markets are both supported via Naked Trading.
