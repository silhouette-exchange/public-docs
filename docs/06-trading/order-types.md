Silhouette currently supports two order types: Market and Limit, with more order types coming soon.

- **Market**: An order that executes immediately at the current market price
- **Limit**: An order that executes at the selected limit price or better

## Coming Soon

- **Shielded TWAP** (Time Weighted Average Price)
- **Shielded VWAP** (Volume Weighted Average Price)
- **Shielded RFQ** (Request for Quote)
- **Shielded RFM** (Request for Match)
